» Enter vehicle name by typing in the search textbox or select from the dropdown selection.



» SELECT REPORT: COMPONENTS PER SPECIFICATIONS

Select and click "Components per Specification(S) to hide or show the "Vehicle Specifications Window.



» Click " + " button to show/hide other details. Click on the checkbox to select vehicle specifications, then click the button to open the "vehicleReport" Page.



» SELECT REPORT: HIGHLIGHTS

Select and click "Highlights" then click the button to open the "http://phpet-web04:888/page/name/vehicleHihgligtsReport?project_id=" Page.



» SELECT REPORT: FEATURES

Select and click "Features" then click the button to open the "vehicleFeaturesReport?" Page.



» SELECT REPORT: COMPONENTS ANALYSIS

Select and click "Component Analysis" then click the button to open the "componentAnalysisReport?" Page.







Report ► Vehicle Comparison

» Click from the Menu, then select the "Vehicle Comparison" under the Report Sub-menu to open the "Vehicle Comparison" Page.



» Select atleast 2 vehicles to compare by clicking in the appropriate checkboxes from the dropdown selections inside the "Vehicle Filter Criteria Box".

Images of the selected vehicles are displayed at the upper box, after the selections are made.



» SELECT REPORT: COMPONENTS PER SPECIFICATIONS

Select and click "Components per Specification(S) to hide or show the "Vehicle Specifications Window.



» Click " + " button to show/hide other details. Click on the checkbox to select vehicle specifications, then click the button to open the "comparisonReport" Page.



» SELECT REPORT: HIGHLIGHTS

Select and click "Highlights" then click the button to open the "highlightsAnalysis" Page.



» SELECT REPORT: FEATURES

Select and click "Features" then click the button to open the "featuresAnalysis" Page.





Report ► Component Analysis Comparison
